TITLE: Connecting to the Corporate Office Wi-Fi
CATEGORY: Network & Remote Access

When working inside a corporate office, you must connect to the secure employee network:

1. Select "HappiestMinds-Secure" from the list of available Wi-Fi networks.
2. When prompted, enter your standard Windows login credentials (email and password).
3. If asked to accept a security certificate, click "Connect" or "Accept".
4. Do NOT connect to "HappiestMinds-Guest" with your corporate device, as it will restrict access to internal company resources.

If your device repeatedly fails to authenticate, try "Forgetting" the network in your Wi-Fi settings and re-entering your credentials.
