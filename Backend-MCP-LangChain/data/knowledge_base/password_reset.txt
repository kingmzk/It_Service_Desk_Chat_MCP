TITLE: How to Reset Your Corporate Password
CATEGORY: Identity & Access Management

If you have forgotten your password or are locked out of your account, follow these steps:

1. Go to the Entra ID Self-Service Password Reset (SSPR) portal.
2. Enter your full corporate email address (e.g., firstname.lastname@happiestminds.com).
3. Complete the multi-factor authentication (MFA) prompt sent to your registered mobile device.
4. Enter your new password. It must be at least 12 characters long, contain an uppercase letter, a number, and a special character.
5. You cannot reuse any of your last 5 passwords.

If you are unable to receive the MFA prompt, please raise a high-priority ticket for manual verification by the IT Security team.
